Privacy Support

Nutrition tracking, kept private.

MacroSight logs your meals, scans labels, and estimates macros from photos — all without sending anything to a server we own.

Your food log lives in your private iCloud database. Apple Health stays with Apple Health. Optional AI estimation uses your own Anthropic key or Apple's on-device model.

iCloud-native

No login. Your entries sync across iPhone, iPad, Mac, and Watch through your iCloud account.

Scan, snap, or search

Barcode lookup, OCR-based label scanning, USDA database search, or AI estimation from a meal photo.

Bring your own AI

Use your Anthropic API key for the cloud Claude model, or toggle Apple's on-device Foundation Model for zero-network estimation.

Apple Health, both ways

Reads your weight and body composition; writes nutrition back to Health so other apps can use it.

Watch + widgets

Today's macros on your wrist and on your Home Screen. Glanceable, no taps required.

No tracking. No ads.

Zero analytics SDKs. No advertising frameworks. No fingerprinting. The privacy report is short on purpose.

What we don't do

  • We never see your food log. It lives in your private CloudKit database.
  • We don't run analytics. There is no Firebase, Mixpanel, Amplitude, or anything else watching you.
  • We don't sell anything to anyone. There is no ad SDK and no attribution framework.
  • We don't ask for accounts, emails, or phone numbers. Sign in with Apple isn't even an option, because there's nothing to sign into.

The full accounting is in our privacy policy.
